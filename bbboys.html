Bindubasini Govt. Boys' High School

Bindubasini Govt. Boys' High School, located in Tangail, Bangladesh, is a prestigious educational institution with a rich history dating back to its establishment in 1880. Originally named Graham English High School, it was later renamed in honor of Bindubasini Chowdhurany, who played a significant role in its development. The school has been a beacon of academic excellence, shaping generations of students with its commitment to quality education and discipline. Over the years, it has grown into a well-respected institution, offering a structured curriculum under the Dhaka Education Board and fostering a dynamic learning environment. With a dedicated team of educators, modern facilities, and a strong emphasis on extracurricular activities such as cricket and football, Bindubasini Govt. Boys' High School ensures holistic development for its students. The school has consistently produced outstanding graduates who have gone on to make significant contributions in various fields. Its motto, "Education is Light," reflects its mission to empower young minds with knowledge and values. The institution has also been recognized as one of the best high schools in Bangladesh, further solidifying its reputation as a center for academic and personal growth. Whether through its rigorous academic programs, vibrant student community, or historical significance, Bindubasini Govt. Boys' High School remains a cornerstone of education in Bangladesh.
